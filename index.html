About North Hills

North Hills Logo

The North Hills logo was inspired by a crest and shield. It was meant to represent the importance of the school and that it was safe for kids at all ages. North Hills is my favorite school. The teachers are always nice and the students as well, it's a public school. It has two floors.



North Hills main building

The North Hills main building is where the middle School is. The middle school is where I am going to currently. It contains the 6th, 7th, and 8th grades. I study in the 7th grade. Next to the main build is the elementary and high school. The elementary is on the right side of the main building. The High school is on the left side.



North Hills Gym Model

The north hills Gym is where we go to attend P.E., Physical Education. It is also where kids play after school to wait for their parents the pick them up. In the Gym building there is also the Orchestra classroom as well. Behind the gym there is a huge field where we play soccer.
